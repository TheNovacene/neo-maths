Coordinates are like GPS for maths! A point on the grid is defined by an x-coordinate (horizontal) and a y-coordinate (vertical).

We write coordinates as (x, y). The grid is divided into four quadrants, each with positive or negative x and y values.

  • Quadrant I: (+x, +y)
  • Quadrant II: (–x, +y)
  • Quadrant III: (–x, –y)
  • Quadrant IV: (+x, –y)
