Vectors: The Language of Direction and Magnitude

🔎 Reflection — What patterns can you notice — and what do you notice about your own thinking?

Vectors describe movement and position in terms of both direction and magnitude. They allow us to precisely represent translations, forces, velocities, and more in the world around us.

How are vectors written? What do their components mean?

🤝 Connection — Whose story does this maths tell, and who does it connect?

From GPS navigation to engineering structures and sports trajectories, vectors are vital in countless real-world contexts. Understanding them helps us interpret and influence the world with accuracy.
