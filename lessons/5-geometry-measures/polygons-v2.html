🟣 Exploring the Interior Angles of Regular Polygons

Use the interactive Polygon Explorer below to investigate how the sum of interior angles changes as the number of sides increases in a regular polygon. Can you discover the formula?

Polygon Explorer Web App by Gerry Docherty

🔎 Reflection — What patterns can you notice — and what do you notice about your own thinking?

Use the interactive tool to draw diagonals from a single vertex and divide each polygon into triangles. Record how many triangles you can form, and use that to calculate the total sum of interior angles for polygons with 3 to 8 sides. What pattern do you notice?

🤝 Connection — Whose story does this maths tell, and who does it connect?

Regular polygons have appeared in architecture and art for thousands of years — from ancient Greek temples to Islamic mosaics. Why do you think these shapes were so popular in different cultures? What role might symmetry and balance play?

🎨 Creativity — How can you make, imagine, or express this in your own way?

How would you visually represent what you’ve learned about interior angles? Could you sketch a poster or create a short video explaining how the number of sides affects the angle sums? What’s the most creative way you can share the pattern?

🔄 Movement — How does this idea move — and how can you put it to work?

This tool helps you experience geometric reasoning through exploration. Try toggling features like vertex labels and the circumcircle. Can you think of how this kind of tool could help in engineering, animation, or architecture?

🌙 Rest — Where’s the space to breathe, pace yourself, and let it settle?

How does your brain respond as you search for patterns and try to form general rules? What strategies help you stay focused or manage frustration when things don’t work right away? Can you feel the satisfaction when a rule “clicks” into place?

🌱 Nutrition · Final Task — What real-world need does this feed?

Using the data from your investigation, write a general formula for the sum of interior angles of a regular polygon with n sides.

Challenge: Can you use this formula to calculate the interior angle of a regular polygon? (Hint: Divide the sum by the number of sides!)
