The Sine Rule and Area of a Triangle — Practice Companion
Pure 1, Trigonometry · Lesson 1

Eight exam-style questions on the sine rule (including the ambiguous case) and the area formula ½ ab sin C. Whenever you're stuck, you have four kinds of help, in this order:

🤔 Socratic prompt — a question to get you thinking.   💡 Hint — a piece of method.   ✅ Check my answer — try as many times as you like.   👀 Worked solution — the full method, unlocked once you've genuinely tried.

Give angles in degrees to 1 decimal place and lengths/areas to 2 decimal places unless the question says otherwise. The aim isn't speed — it's understanding.
