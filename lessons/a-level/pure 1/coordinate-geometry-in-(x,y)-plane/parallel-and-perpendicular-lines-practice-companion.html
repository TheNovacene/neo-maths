Eight exam-style questions on parallel and perpendicular lines. Whenever you're stuck, you have four kinds of help, in this order:

🤔 Socratic prompt — a question to get you thinking (tap again for a more specific one).   💡 Hint — a piece of method.   ✅ Check my answer — try as many times as you like; you'll get diagnostic feedback.   👀 Worked solution — the full method, unlocked once you've genuinely tried.

Give gradients as fractions or decimals (both accepted). For "general form" answers, use integer coefficients with the leading term positive, e.g. 3x − y − 5 = 0.
