Problem Solving with Straight Lines — Practice Companion
Pure 1, Coordinate Geometry · Lesson 3

Eight exam-style multi-step problems drawing on the whole Coordinate Geometry toolkit. Whenever you're stuck, you have four kinds of help, in this order:

🤔 Socratic prompt — a question to get you thinking.   💡 Hint — a piece of method.   ✅ Check my answer — try as many times as you like.   👀 Worked solution — the full method, unlocked once you've genuinely tried.

These questions are bigger than the ones in Lessons 1 and 2 — they ask you to combine tools. Use the working box to lay out each step. Final answers go in the answer box.
